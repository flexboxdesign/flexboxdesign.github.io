Portfolio

Pinoy Kitchen & Grill:

When developing a web presence for Pinoy Kitchen & Grill we wanted to encapsulate exactly what the restaraunt has to offer. Utilizing HTML5, CSS3 and basic Javascript we created an informative home page that allows customers to view everything the restaraunt has to offer. Using vibrant colors based on Filipino heritage, we designed a custom banner responsive to both mobile and desktop viewing.

Jesse Thomas: Art Gallery

Our vision for Jesse Thomas' portfolio was to design something that was simple to the core. We wanted to make sure that there was nothing too intrusive that might take away from the quality of the art he created. Utilizing Javascript and Jquery elements, we customized a lightbox gallery that allows users to scroll and navigate between photos. Additionally, it allows you to zoom in and see the details of the art down to the pixel.
